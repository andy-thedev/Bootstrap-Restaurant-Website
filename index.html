house specialsHaving trouble deciding? Here are our most beloved-dishes.

1 Signature Smoke-BurgerA home-made, smoked, beef-patty burger with an egg on top. Satisfying the cravings of the most ambitious burger-lovers, this delicacy has been one of our signature dishes for many, many years.

2 Alfredo Crab-Penne Pasta BowlSweet crabmeat in a creamy garlic Alfredo sauce, mixed together with a bed of penne pasta. This single bowl will instantly satisfy all of your creamy cravings of the week.

3 Shrimp Parmesan Linguini PastaA fresh, garlic parmesan linguini pasta, with jumbo shrimps, for that extra soft, popping texture. For anyone looking for a light, fresh meal, this dish is the perfect choice.

4 Mama's Fudgy Cocoa BrowniesA home-made, deep, chocolate fudge brownie to top off your well-deserved meal. This sweet delicacy is our local favourite dessert, unlike any other brownie elsewhere.
